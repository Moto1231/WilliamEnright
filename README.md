# WilliamEnright

## Business Technology Advisor

Executive-level technology guidance and fractional CTO capabilities for organizations that need better alignment between business objectives and technology decisions.

William Enright helps leadership teams understand technology options, avoid unnecessary complexity, and make decisions that strengthen the business.

## Core Philosophy

Technology should support the business — not force the business to adapt unnecessarily to technology.

The goal is not more technology. The goal is better business outcomes.
